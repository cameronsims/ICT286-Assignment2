How to Search

Click on the "catalog" menu option, there you will be given two ways to search the Database for items. Searching by Catergory or searching by keywords, both can be used seperately or together. Searching with "None" as a catergory will return all items. If no items have been queried the catalog entries will be empty, and no items will be visible until returned.
